# Pathway Engineering

## Overview
This skill focuses on the metabolic engineering design cycle, from pathway selection to enzyme optimization.

## Capabilities
- **Design Pathway**: Propose metabolic routes to a target molecule.
- **Optimize Enzymes**: Suggest mutations or homologs to improve flux.

## Usage
- "Design a pathway to produce n-butanol in yeast."
- "Optimize the limonene synthase enzyme for higher activity."

## Example: Pathway Design
Target: Lycopene
Precursors: IPP, DMAPP
Genes: crtE, crtB, crtI
Host: E. coli
